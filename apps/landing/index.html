Your office foosball league, minus the spreadsheet.

Foos & Friends tracks matches, ELO rankings and seasons for your crew — from the first serve to the final table. Free, mobile-first and ready before your next break.

Start playing See how it works

Rankings that mean something

Every match moves the table. Ratings are ELO-based, so beating the top seed pays more than farming the intern. Separate 1v1 and 2v2 tables keep singles specialists and doubles dynasties honest.

Log a match in seconds

Pick players, tap the winner, done — before the ball stops rolling. Play 1v1 or 2v2, and let smart team balancing build fair sides when skill levels don't match. Every result lands in a full match history.

Seasons keep it fresh

Start a new season and everyone returns to 1200 — last quarter's champion has to defend the title from scratch. Player profiles chart every rating swing, and the all-time table never forgets who really runs the office.

How it works

  1. 1

    Rally your crew

    Create a group and share one invite link. Signing in is a magic link to your email — no passwords to forget.

  2. 2

    Play and log it

    After each game, tap in the result — winner first. 1v1 or 2v2, balanced teams on request.

  3. 3

    Climb the table

    Ratings update instantly. End the season, crown a champion, reset to 1200 and go again.
